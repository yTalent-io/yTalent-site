Mission, vision and values

Our mission is to provide data transparency within recruitment for hiring your next commercial talents, so you’ll be able to get a more predictable hiring process without relying on gut feelings and the element of guessing. By including several data driven models and leveraging AI, startups and scaleups are significantly reducing the time spent on hiring processes and are able to employ new talents that drive their business to new heights.

Curiosity

Curiosity is one of the most valuable traits that lead to an excellent hire. Curious people will figure out solutions to problems on their own, and let’s face it, there can be a lot of issues and challenges in startups.

Coachability

Coachability means ingesting feedback from peers and managers, and actively using that feedback to better your performance. Top performers are learning something new every day, and they are shameless when it comes to asking for coaching.

Positive attitude

The chances of you encountering challenges in a startup is around 100%. Especially when you work in very small teams. If something goes south or something unexpected happens, it’s crucial that you can continue to push through.

Culture

yTalent strives to create a culture that shows honesty and trust within our team. We are thrilled about our startup and scale-up customers and we believe that through precise communication and trust we can make their business grow stronger. We believe that having the right mentality is what brings the most value to our business. This means that we keep ourselves in a state of learning and innovating our processes with the aim of changing the status quo.
