Digital Marketing Inter

Are you curious and ambitious to create an actual change in an industry that stayed the same for decades? Do you want to create engaging content and new marketing strategies? Then we have the perfect internship for you right here at yTalent!



yTalent is a recruitment agency that wants to change the way the industry works today. We are making recruitment predictable by using data on both hard and soft skills using AI to work. You will be part of a small team full of dedicated employees with the will to succeed. Our work environment is informal, and we want to make our startup journey as fun as possible.


Who are you?


We are looking for a Marketing Intern who is curious enough to come up with new ideas for solving problems. You are enthusiastic about learning and developing your skill set. You are not afraid to test different marketing strategies to raise brand awareness and drive growth.


Requirements


Proficiency in English.
Marketing experience
High level of coachability.
High level of curiosity
Eagerness to learn and adapt in an ever-changing, fast-growing environment.


Bonus points


Native Danish language skills
Located in or around Copenhagen
Startup experience


What you’ll be doing?


Our innovative and open work culture gives you the opportunity to apply your skills in many different areas of Digital Marketing, e.g., you will have the chance to use your skills in creating content, budget planning, and optimizing our marketing funnels as part of our effort to increase brand awareness. You will also assist with other ad hoc marketing tasks - primarily our digital presence on our website and social media and maintaining and developing our marketing material.


Position details


You’ll work closely with the Founder and CEO Jakob and our current marketing team. We don’t expect you to be an expert in a very defined marketing area, but we wouldn’t mind if you were :)
The internship is an unpaid position (working hours are 37 hours) and extends over 3-6 months. Details are agreed in connection with interviews.
A very flexible place to work - both time and location.
